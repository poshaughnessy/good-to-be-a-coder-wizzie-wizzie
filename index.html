Why it's good to be a

Coder

By Peter O'Shaughnessy, for:

My road to code

First coded on my brothers' ZX Spectrum

Took a degree in Computer Science

Now been a developer for 10 years

It's great being a coder, because...

...it's creative and fun

...you can create your own games, apps, & websites

...computers are everywhere & becoming ever more important

...without coders there would be no

  • World Wide Web
  • Video games
  • Mobile phones
  • Online social networking
  • Digital cameras
  • Digital music
  • Digital video
  • Calculators
  • E-books
  • Credit cards
  • ATMs
  • Barcodes
  • Modern medicine
  • Modern transport
  • Space travel
  • ...

...technology is moving so fast

Gestural interfaces (Leap Motion)

Smart watches (Pebble)

Smart glasses (Google Glass)

3D printing (Makerbot)

Mind control (brainwave sensors)

...coders get to play with gadgets!

...start while you're young

“Programming is a language skill and the best coders learn young enough to become natives”
CoderDojo

...there's high demand

“We simply cannot hire enough computer scientists and this seems madness at a time of such high youth unemployment”
Ian Livingstone, Life President, Eidos Interactive

...it's well paid

“If you want to have a high-paying job on graduation day, study computer science”
Network World

...it's an exciting time right here in London

“London's Tech City is Europe's Fastest Growing Tech Cluster”
businessdawes.com

...computer experts are cool in Hollywood now!

But most importantly...

...computers are changing the world

“Do we direct technology, or do we let ourselves be directed by it and those who have mastered it?

... Choose the former and you gain access to the control panel of civilisation.”

Program or be Programmed, Douglas Rushkoff

In other words...

Being able to code

gives you power

that you can use for good

Have fun!
